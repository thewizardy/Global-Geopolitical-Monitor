📌 Overview

Haiti continues to experience political instability, gang violence, and humanitarian crises, severely impacting civilian life and national security.

⚔ Military & Security Actions

  • Local and national authorities are engaging in operations to dismantle gang networks.
  • Security forces conduct raids in urban and rural hotspots.
  • International support is provided for stabilization and peacekeeping efforts.

🤝 Diplomatic Engagements

  • Engagement with the UN and international organizations for humanitarian aid and security support.
  • Regional diplomatic efforts to stabilize governance and prevent escalation.

🌍 Ongoing Military Operations

  • Gang Conflict Operations: Coordinated efforts by national authorities and international forces to control gang-dominated areas.

What You Need to Know:

  • Gangs control significant portions of Port-au-Prince and other cities.
  • Raids are ongoing to reclaim urban areas and restore law enforcement control.
  • International organizations assist in humanitarian relief and security coordination.
  • Political instability complicates sustained military operations.
  • Civilians face displacement and disruptions due to ongoing clashes.
