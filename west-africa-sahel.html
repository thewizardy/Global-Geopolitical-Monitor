📌 Overview

The West Africa / Sahel region faces persistent instability due to jihadist insurgencies, armed groups, and intercommunal conflicts. Countries affected include Mali, Niger, Burkina Faso, and surrounding areas, with ongoing humanitarian crises.

⚔ Military & Security Actions

  • National and regional forces conducting counter-insurgency operations against groups like Boko Haram and Al-Qaeda affiliates.
  • Joint military operations by the G5 Sahel countries to stabilize border regions.
  • International peacekeeping missions supporting local security and humanitarian relief.

🤝 Diplomatic Engagements

  • Regional cooperation initiatives between Sahel countries to enhance security and intelligence sharing.
  • Engagement with the United Nations and EU for conflict resolution and humanitarian support.

🌍 Ongoing Military Operations

  • Counter-Insurgency Operations: Military campaigns targeting jihadist groups and armed militias across Mali, Niger, and Burkina Faso.

What You Need to Know:

  • Jihadist groups remain active in northern and central Sahel regions, attacking civilians and military forces.
  • G5 Sahel countries coordinate cross-border operations to disrupt insurgent movements.
  • International peacekeeping forces assist in stabilizing contested areas and protecting humanitarian missions.
  • Civilians continue to face displacement, food insecurity, and attacks on villages.
  • Political instability in several Sahel nations complicates sustained security operations.
