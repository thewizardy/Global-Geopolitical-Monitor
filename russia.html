📌 Overview

Russia is a nuclear superpower with significant military capability and strategic depth across Eurasia. Its geopolitical posture emphasizes regional dominance, NATO counterbalancing, and the promotion of a multipolar global order.

⚔ External Military Actions & Deployments

  • Ongoing large-scale war operations in Ukraine.
  • Military presence and influence operations in parts of Africa.
  • Strategic air patrols near NATO airspace.
  • Naval deployments in the Black Sea and Arctic regions.
  • Joint military exercises with strategic partners.
  • Arctic militarization and Northern Fleet expansion.

🌍 Ongoing Military Operations

  • Ukraine War Front: Russia continues sustained offensive operations in eastern and southern Ukraine.

What You Need to Know:

  • Russian forces are advancing in key sectors of eastern Ukraine, particularly in Donetsk.
  • Missile and drone strikes continue targeting Ukrainian energy and military infrastructure.
  • Ukraine has intensified long-range drone strikes inside Russian territory.
  • Western military aid to Ukraine remains ongoing.
  • Arctic Military Expansion: Russia strengthens its military presence in the Arctic region.

What You Need to Know:

  • Russia has reopened and modernized several Soviet-era Arctic bases.
  • New radar systems and air defense platforms have been deployed along the Northern Sea Route.
  • Increased submarine patrols reported in Arctic waters.
  • The Arctic is viewed as strategically vital for energy reserves and trade routes.
  • NATO Border Tensions: Military buildup continues along Russia–NATO frontiers.

What You Need to Know:

  • NATO has expanded troop deployments in Eastern Europe.
  • Russia has reinforced forces in Kaliningrad and western regions.
  • Large-scale military exercises are being conducted on both sides.
  • Airspace interceptions between NATO and Russian aircraft have increased.
  • Wagner & Mercenary Activity: Russian-linked private military networks continue overseas operations.

What You Need to Know:

  • Wagner-linked forces remain active in parts of Africa and the Middle East.
  • Russian paramilitary structures have been reorganized under tighter state control.
  • Operations focus on resource security and political influence.
  • Nuclear Posture Updates: Russia signals strategic deterrence readiness.

What You Need to Know:

  • Strategic missile forces remain on high alert status.
  • Modernization of intercontinental ballistic missile systems continues.
  • Public statements emphasize nuclear deterrence against NATO expansion.
