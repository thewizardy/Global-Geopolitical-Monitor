📌 Overview

India is a rising regional power in South Asia with growing economic, military, and technological influence. Its strategic focus includes maritime security, border defense, and expanding international partnerships.

⚔ External Military Actions & Deployments

  • Active naval deployments in the Indian Ocean and Bay of Bengal.
  • Joint military exercises with Indo-Pacific partners: Japan, Australia, US.
  • Counterterrorism operations in border regions and international training missions.
  • Peacekeeping and security operations under UN mandates.

🌍 Ongoing Military Operations

  • Maritime Security: India maintains active naval presence in the Indian Ocean and Bay of Bengal.

What You Need to Know:

  • India chairs the Indian Ocean Naval Symposium to strengthen regional maritime security cooperation.
  • Joint naval exercises conducted with the US, Japan, and Australia.
  • Counter-piracy and freedom of navigation missions ongoing.
  • Border security operations remain active along northern and western frontiers.
  • Air Force modernization and strategic missile programs continue.

🤝 Major Diplomatic Engagements

  • Strategic partnership with the United States, Japan, and Australia (QUAD).
  • Participation in BRICS and South Asian regional forums.
  • Maritime diplomacy via Indian Ocean Naval Symposium (IONS).
  • Energy and trade agreements with Middle Eastern and African partners.

🏠 Internal Security & Domestic Actions

  • Counterterror operations in northern and northeastern states.
  • Border security reinforcement along Pakistan and China borders.
  • Cybersecurity and strategic information defense programs.
  • Modernization of Indian armed forces and military technology.

💰 Strategic Economic Tools

  • Export of software, technology, and military equipment.
  • Energy diplomacy, including imports of oil and gas from strategic partners.
  • Investment in infrastructure and defense industries to boost economic resilience.
  • Regional trade and security cooperation agreements.

🛡 Military Capability

  • Large conventional army with modernized infantry and armored units.
  • Growing blue-water navy and air force capabilities.
  • Strategic missile and nuclear deterrent programs.
  • Cyber warfare and intelligence capabilities under expansion.

📊 Strategic Tension Index

Global Engagement Level: Medium

Regional Conflict Risk (Pakistan & China borders): Moderate
